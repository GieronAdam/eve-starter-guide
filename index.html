Best Beginner Activities in EVE Online

Exploration

Exploration is one of the best ways to make early ISK. A cheap frigate can already earn surprisingly good money in relic and data sites. It is also one of the best choices for solo players because the startup cost is low and the learning curve is fair.

Mining

Mining is simple and beginner-friendly. It is perfect if you want a more relaxed start, and it becomes even better if you plan to multibox or join a corporation later.

Gas Harvesting

Gas huffing is a strong early-income path for players who like the mining style but want better payout potential. Wormhole gas sites can be very profitable even for newer players.

Faction Warfare

If you want combat from the start, faction warfare is one of the best options in EVE Online. It gives you PvP experience, LP income and a reason to fly cheap frigates without fear.

How to Make Your First ISK

New players often ask how to make their first 10M, 50M or 100M ISK. The answer depends on your preferred play style, but the most reliable beginner methods are exploration, mining and faction warfare. Exploration tends to scale the fastest, mining is consistent and relaxed, and faction warfare is ideal if you want action together with income.

The best approach is to pick one activity for your first week instead of trying everything at once. That makes it easier to train the right skills, buy the right ship and build momentum early.

Recommended Starter Ships

  • Heron – beginner exploration frigate.
  • Venture – first mining and gas ship.
  • Tristan – strong entry combat frigate.
  • Magnate / Imicus / Probe – good exploration choices depending on faction.
