Best Beginner Activities in EVE Online

Exploration

Exploration is one of the best ways to make early ISK. A cheap frigate can already earn surprisingly good money in relic and data sites.

Gas Harvesting

Gas huffing is a great option if you enjoy a mining-like style but want better income potential, especially in wormhole space.

Faction Warfare

If you want combat early, faction warfare is one of the best paths. You can learn PvP, earn loyalty points and start fighting quickly.

Mining

Mining is simple, relaxing and works well for players who want a calm start, especially if you plan to multibox or join a corporation later.

Recommended Starter Ships

  • Heron – great beginner exploration frigate.
  • Venture – best first ship for mining and gas harvesting.
  • Tristan – strong early combat choice for new players.
  • Magnate / Imicus / Probe – good exploration options depending on faction.

Quick Starter Advice

  • Do the tutorial and career agents first.

  • Exploration is usually one of the fastest early-money methods.
  • Join a beginner-friendly corporation when you feel ready.
