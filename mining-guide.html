EVE Online Mining Guide for Beginners

Mining is one of the easiest ways to start earning steady ISK in EVE Online, especially if you like a calmer play style or plan to multibox in the future.

Start EVE with 1M SP

Why Mining Is Good for New Players

Mining is simple to understand and easy to enter. It works well for players who want a low-stress start and a stable way to learn the game. It is also one of the most natural activities to expand later into hauling, reprocessing, manufacturing and multibox gameplay.

How to Start Mining

  1. Finish the tutorial and career agents.
  2. Train the basic mining skills needed for a Venture.
  3. Mine in safer space first while learning the interface and routines.
  4. Upgrade your fit and decide whether you prefer ore, gas or a future industrial path.

How Mining Makes ISK

Mining income is usually steadier than exploration, though it may scale more slowly at the start. That makes it especially appealing if you enjoy routines, fleets or a gradual progression path. New players often combine mining with market sales, refining and later industry to improve profitability.

Mining also becomes much stronger when you add more characters later. That is one of the reasons so many long-term EVE players start with mining or return to it once they begin multiboxing.
